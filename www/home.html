The source of txt / csv data from this quran text mining application is obtained from:

1. We download simple text from Tanzil Quran Documents
2. The Indonesian translation of the Quran is taken from http://www.qurandatabase.org
3. Data The names of the suras in the Quran are taken from the website of the Ministry of Religion of the Republic of Indonesia, then we process them into a txt file Quran Kemenag
4. For juz data in the Quran we take references from https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Juz%27
